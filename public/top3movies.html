My favourite Movies of all time

Top 3

1. Joker

The acting and storyline makes this movie simply spectacular.
It touched me in so many ways as you really feel sorry for Arthur.

2. Ace Ventura 1 & 2

I know! These are 2 seperate movies, but I'm always conflicted on which one is best.
I often find myself loving one or the other over the other, so both have to go in.
Really funny films

3. Cool Runnings

A Jamaican team goes to Calgary (one of the coldest places on earth) to compete in the olympics as bobsledders.
Based on a true story, although slightly changed for comedy, I find many belly laughs with this film along with a teary eye at the end.
And this is why it comes in as my 3rd most favourite film of all time.

These films might seem boring in many ways to some people, but as I grew up in the 90's, and in much simpler times, I find that these were the peak of some of my favourite actors and films.
And therefore, I'm right :).
